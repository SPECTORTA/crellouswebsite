Kobi is an 18 year old creative who has always been a natural storyteller. Growing up next to Byron Bay he quickly grew fond of the natural world and beautiful landscape that surrounded him, particularly the ocean. It was only at 13 years old that Kobi bought his first camera and immediately fell in love with both photography and videography.
Having over 5 years experience sharing his perspectives from behind the lens, he is best known for his documentary style short films. His passion for all variations of content creation has partially shaped him into the person he is today. Currently working as a freelance videographer and photographer.
